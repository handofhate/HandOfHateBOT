Changelog

[1.1.1] - 2025-04-13
--------------------

⚙️ Minor Update
- Bumped version to 1.1.1

🔧 Configuration Cleanup
- Moved hardcoded channel name ("handofhate") to config.twitch.channel
- Introduced config.twitch.streamerName to customize display name in !uptime responses

🛠️ Bug Fix
